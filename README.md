# 📊 Data Analysis & Pricing Strategy Project

## 📌 Overview
This project focuses on data analysis, visualization, and data-driven decision-making using multiple datasets related to sales, inventory, competitor insights, and pricing strategies.

The goal is to extract meaningful insights and support business decisions such as:
- Sales trend analysis  
- Inventory optimization  
- Competitive pricing strategies  
- Demand forecasting  

---

## 📁 Project Structure
 ├── Historical_Sales.ipynb
├── Competitor_data.ipynb
├── Inventory_Health.ipynb
├── Pricing Strategy & Data-Driven Decision Making.ipynb
├── Pricing_Data.ipynb
---

## 📊 Key Components

### 1. 📈 Historical Sales Analysis
- Analyzes past sales data  
- Identifies trends and seasonality  
- Helps in forecasting future demand  

### 2. 🏪 Inventory Health Analysis
- Evaluates stock levels  
- Identifies overstock and stockouts  
- Supports inventory optimization  

### 3. 🏷️ Pricing Data Analysis
- Examines pricing patterns  
- Identifies optimal price ranges  
- Supports revenue maximization  

### 4. 🧾 Competitor Analysis
- Compares competitor pricing  
- Identifies market positioning  
- Helps in competitive strategy building  

### 5. 🧠 Pricing Strategy & Decision Making
- Combines insights from all datasets  
- Suggests data-driven pricing strategies  
- Supports business decision-making
